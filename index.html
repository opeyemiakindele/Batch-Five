About the award

This edition of Awards Ceremony will cover 2017 BATCH A of Serving CORPS MEMBERS in the state. Nomination process will start on January 2017 and the entries will be shortlisted for Voting process after nomination has been accepted by the nominee. Winners will not be announced until the night of the award ceremony. Kwara State Corpers Awards 2018 is prestigious with a real focus on outstanding Corps Members and firms/individuals that have played a successful role to the success of the NYSC scheme.
